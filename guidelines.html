Computer Room Rules for Everyone

Quiet Space

Keep your voice low

Speak softly and avoid loud conversations. The computer room is a shared study area and quiet focus helps everyone.

No Food

Avoid food and drinks

Food and beverages can damage the computers and keyboards. Bring only water in a closed container if needed.

Respect Equipment

Use devices gently

Handle mice, keyboards, and screens with care. Report any broken or malfunctioning equipment to staff immediately.

Log Out

Sign out after your session

Always log out of your account and close applications before leaving to protect your privacy and prepare the station for the next user.

Time Limit

Follow the usage limit

Respect the time limits set for computer use so others can also work and learn without waiting for an available workstation.

Be Helpful

Ask staff for assistance

If you are unsure about the rules or need help using a computer, please ask library staff. They are there to support you.
