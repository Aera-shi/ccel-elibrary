Welcome to Caloocan City E-Library

Empowering the community through accessible knowledge, digital resources, and lifelong learning opportunities for all ages.

Mission

To provide inclusive access to information and promote literacy in the digital age.

Vision

A modern, innovative, and community-centered digital library for everyone.

Facilities

Lounge

Anyone who enters the Caloocan City E-Library will be accommodated in a lounge where every reader will experience the feeling of having a welcoming second home. Anyone may take a rest in the area and newspapers are available for leisurely reading.

View more images

Reading and Recreation Hub

The modernization of the E-Library has a traditional heart. The physical collections of the library and its sections such as Filipiniana, Textbooks, Theses, and the like are still available for room use.
