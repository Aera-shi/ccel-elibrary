Welcome to Caloocan City E-Library

Empowering the community through accessible knowledge, digital resources, and lifelong learning opportunities for all ages.

Mission & Vision

🎯

Mission

To provide library and information resources to the citizens in support of the educational plans and programs of the city.

🌟

Vision

To enhance the educational, social and cultural development of the community through library and information services.

Facilities

Lounge

Anyone who enters the Caloocan City E-Library will be accommodated in a lounge where every reader will experience the feeling of having a welcoming second home. Anyone may take a rest in the area and newspapers are available for leisurely reading for everyone's use.
View more images

Reading and Recreation Hub

The modernization of the E-Library has a traditional heart. The physical collections of the library and its sections such as Filipiniana, Textbooks, Theses, and the like are still available for room use..

View more images

Children's Section

The E-Library also caters the children, as books for them are available in this section, and they can also learn while playing as there are also toys that they can use.
View more images

Computer Hub

The core of an E-Library is, of course, the computer. They are available in this section. Whatever the physical limitation of the library can be compensated for by computers as they have digital resources or e-resources in the form of digital articles or books, and even magazines, available on the Internet. The e-resources are available for everyone to use, whether for research or general reference.
