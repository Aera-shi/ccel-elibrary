Library Services

Discover Our Services

Explore the support available at Caloocan City E-Library through service cards, photo highlights, QR access, and easy instructions for each service.

Service Overview

📚

Library Information Services

Get help with catalog search, book recommendations, membership registration, and research support from our information desk.

👧

Children's Library Service

Enjoy a playful reading space with storybooks, learning toys, and guided activities designed for young readers.

🎲

Board Games

Borrow and use board games in the library for learning, social interaction, and relaxed play with friends and family.

💻

Computer & Internet Access

Use our computer stations for online research, document preparation, digital learning, and responsible internet browsing.

Service Photo Gallery

Scan to Access Services

Use your phone to scan the QR code and open our mobile service portal instantly. Request assistance, book a computer session, or learn more about each service.

If your phone does not recognize QR codes automatically, use any QR scanner app or your camera app.

How to Avail Each Service

Library Information Services

Approach the main information desk, ask a staff member about your needs, or scan the QR code to send a request online.

Children's Library Service

Bring your child to the children's section, choose books or activities, and ask staff for storytime schedules.

Board Games

Request available games at the circulation desk, reserve a table, and enjoy group play while following library rules.

Computer & Internet Access

Register at the computer hub, show your ID, and sign in to use a workstation for online research, schoolwork, or document printing.
