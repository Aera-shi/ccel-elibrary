Library Rules and Regulations

Computer Room Rules for Everyone

REGISTRATION

Kindly fill up the registration form and log book

Scan the displayed QR code at the computer laboratory and log in before using the computer.

TIME

Maximum 2 hours per request

Clients are allowed to use the computer for a maximum of 2 hours per request.

TRANSACTIONS

E-Government and Academic Purposes

Computer may be also used for e-government services and other academic purposes.

NO FOOD AND DRINK

Foods and drinks are not allowed in the area

Food and beverages can damage the computers and keyboards. Bring only water in a closed container if needed.

BE QUIET

Respect others and work quietly

Keep conversations to a minimum and avoid disturbing other users.

ASSISTANCE

Do not unplug and move any computer equipment.

If you need help using a computer, please ask the library staff.

COMPUTER SETTINGS

Do not change any computer settings.

Only authorized personnel are allowed to modify computer settings.

INSTALLATION AND DELETION

Installing and deleting computer software and applications are prohibited.

Only authorized personnel are allowed to install and delete computer softwares.

IT SUPPORT

Kindly report any computer problems/ concern to the IT Support Staff or Library Staff.

Approach the IT Support Staff or Library Staff for assistance.

ONLINE GAMES

Playing and streaming online games are strictly prohibited.

Violations of this rule may result in disciplinary action.

SAVINGS FILES

Use your own personal account (gmail and google drive) or in the computer desktop folder for saving your files.

Avoid using flashdrives, ask for assistance if needed.
