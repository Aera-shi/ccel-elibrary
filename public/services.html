Children's Library Service


Provides a dedicated space for children featuring storybooks, educational materials, and learning toys to foster reading habits and creativity.

🎲

Board Games


Allows patrons to borrow and enjoy board and recreational games within the library, promoting learning, social interaction, and relaxation.

💻

Computer & Internet Access


Offers public access to computers for research, online resources, document preparation, and other computer related works ensuring responsible and academic use in accordance with library policies.

📶

Free Wi-Fi


Offers free Wi-Fi access to all library users, supporting research, online learning, and digital connectivity

🖨️

Free Printing


Provides free printing services available for up to 7 pages per user to assist with your study and research needs.

🔍

Research Assistance


Get help from librarians and staffs for research, interviews and assignments.

🏛️

E-Government Services


As the transactions with the government are digitizing, the e-library is open to anyone who would like to avail the digital transactions with various government agencies such as SSS, Pag-IBIG, and Caloocan City Online Services.
